SnowTrackr - low fidelity snowfall tracking device

[Devpost] [Github]

SnowTrackr is a low fidelity snowfall warning system that consolidates variable sensors to send out SMS messages during adverse snowfall conditions. We embedded two systems: an Arduino system, which recorded variables such as temperature, weight, and a video camera with an OpenCV image processor to measure the proportional volume of snowfall. We wired our two systems together through Node-RED and connected the Twilio SMS API to send notifications during particular snowfall conditions.
