Website Design Inspiration

The main inspiration for the design of this portfolio website was my profound passion for exploration and nature. Having grown up in Calgary and Vancouver, I had a sort of familial attachment to the natural world. Nature reminds me of joyful times spent with family in Banff; the arduous journeys I've spent with friends on Grouse Mountain; and calm excursions on the Stanley Park Seawall. The colour scheme is meant to mimic a gradual transition from the peaks of mountains to the deepest depths of oceans. This representation mimics how nature has nurtured me in all aspects of my life and helped forge my identity.
